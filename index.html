The Best Pepperoni Pizza Ever!

Cheesy, crispy, and topped with spicy perfection

Recipe Overview: Prep 15 min | Cook 20 min | Serves 4

Recipe Name Pepperoni Pizza
Ingredients
  • 1 pre-made pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella
  • 15–20 pepperoni slices
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp oregano
Steps
  1. Preheat oven to 450°F (230°C).
  2. Roll out pizza dough on a lightly floured surface.
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Top with mozzarella and pepperoni slices.
  5. Brush edges with olive oil and sprinkle oregano.
  6. Bake 15–20 minutes until golden brown.
Allergens Contains gluten and dairy.
Nutrition Info Per slice: 250 cal | 12g protein | 28g carbs | 10g fat
Tips & Variations Try adding mushrooms, onions, or jalapeños for extra flavor!
